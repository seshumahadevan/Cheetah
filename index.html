The Speedy Cheetah

Fastest Land Animal

The cheetah can accelerate from 0 to 60 miles per hour in just a few seconds. Its slender body, flexible spine, and powerful legs make it the ultimate sprinter of the animal kingdom.

Unique Spots

Each cheetah has a unique pattern of spots, similar to human fingerprints. These spots help camouflage them in grassy environments while hunting.

Habitat

Cheetahs mainly live in African savannas, preferring open plains where they can use their speed to chase down prey.
